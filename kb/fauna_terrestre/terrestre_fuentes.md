## Grupo 1: Identidad, Manejo y Producción por Especie

### Aves
- **FAO Small-Scale Poultry Production** (openknowledge.fao.org): manejo familiar de aves, razas criollas, sistemas de traspatio en países en desarrollo.
- **LRRD/CIPAV — Family Poultry in Central America**: https://www.lrrd.cipav.org.co/lrrd11/3/mal113.htm — Produccion familiar de pollo criollo en Guatemala y LA.
- **Tandfonline 2023 — Indigenous Chicken Productivity and Resilience**: https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/full/10.1080/09712119.2023.2228374 — revision de productividad de gallinas nativas en tropico.

- **PMC6617375 — Layer Pullet Stocking Densities**: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC6617375/ — densidades maximas ponedoras.
- **Livestocking.net — Floor Space Broilers/Layers**: https://www.livestocking.net/floor-space-requirements-broilers — espacios por ave.
- **PMC9560488 — Broiler tropical semi-arid**: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC9560488/ — densidad broiler en calor.
- **ECHO Community — Quail Production**: https://www.echocommunity.org/resources/3737ffce-c356-46ab-9b1f-35ae8dfcd22c — codorniz sistema tropical.
- **PMC10113653 — Japanese Quail Tropical Tamil Nadu**: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC10113653/ — rendimiento codorniz tropico.

- **NAERLS Nigeria — Guinea Fowl Production Extension**: https://naerls.gov.ng/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/The-Production-of-Guinea-Fowl-in-Nigeria.pdf — manual produccion pintada.
- **Agrodok 33 — Duck Keeping in the Tropics**: https://journeytoforever.org/farm_library/AD33.pdf — crianza de patos en tropicos (Agromisa/CTA).
- **Cornell University — Duck Housing and Management**: https://www.vet.cornell.edu/animal-health-diagnostic-center/programs/duck-research-laboratory/duck-housing-and-management — manejo pato Pekin.
- **ResearchGate — Muscovy Duck Production**: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/330587549 — perfil genetico y produccion pato Muscovy.
- **Springer 2007 — Muscovy Congo**: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11250-007-4235-0 — pato Muscovy sistemas moderados tropicales.
- **FAO — Geese for Meat Production (y4359e)**: https://www.fao.org/4/y4359e/y4359e0b.htm — produccion ganso FAO.
- **Springer — Global Waterfowl Stocking Rate Review**: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11250-023-03835-6 — revision densidad aves acuaticas.
- **PSU Extension — Small Flock Turkey**: https://extension.psu.edu/small-flock-turkey-production — produccion pavo traspatio.
- **ATTRA — Pastured Turkey Production**: https://attra.ncat.org/publication/pastured-turkey-production/ — pavo en pastoreo.
